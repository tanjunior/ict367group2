Controls:

Controls for VR

  • Move vr controller to move hands
  • Press left/right grip to grab on Interactables (Highlighted objects)
  • Y to pause
  • B toggle hologram

Controls for PC

  • [W] to accelerate
  • [Left and right click] to change gear
  • [H] for handbrake
  • [space-bar] for braking
  • [A] and [D] to turn the steering wheel and
  • [tab] toggle hologram
